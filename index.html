Kambata Development Network

Building sustainable communities in Ethiopia

Kambata Development Network is a US 501(c)(3) non-profit organization founded on March 24, 2001 in Maryland, U.S. by community members who are most concerned about the current environmental, social, and economic crises affecting the Kambata population in Southern Ethiopia. Our organization focuses on fighting famines, chronic poverty, youth unemployment, adult illiteracy, social discrimination of women and artisans, environmental degradation and HIV/AIDS.

Local efforts

Assisting local efforts in the construction of bridges, roads, water wells, schools, health posts, and other rural infrastructure in the Kambata region.

Workshops

Organizing educational seminars, workshops, and conferences from time to time. Promoting rural technology including improvement of rudimentary enset decorticating devices.

Improve living standards

To improve the standard of living, we have undertaken various development programs (resettlement, health services, water, education, soil conservation, road construction, etc.) in the region.
